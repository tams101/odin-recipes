Tasty Lasagna

Description

A lasagna that is easy to make and super tasty!

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 180 ml Beef Stock
  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 tinned tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Mixed Herbs
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • White Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  1. Finely chop the onions, garlic and mushrooms
  2. Fry the mince until brown and remove from the heat
  3. Fry the onions and garlic until lightly browned
  4. Add the mushrooms and the cooked mince back to the pan
  5. Add the stock, tinned tomates and herbs. Simmer for 30 minutes
  6. In a deep oven proof dish start with a layer of the tomato base followed by a layer of lasagna sheets and then top with the white sauce. Continue until all sauces are used.
  7. Sprinkle with the grated cheese and bake in the oven for 45 minutes until golden brown
